My field is Theory of Condensed Matter Physics. I am interested in theoretical investigation and numerical exploration of emergent quantum phenomena. This includes:


  1. Density Matrix Renormalization Group(DMRG)

  • Momentum Space DMRG and Quantum Chemistry Calculation

  • Quantum Transfer-Matrix Renormalization Group(free TMRG code available)

  • Pace-Keeping DMRG


  1. High Temperature Superconductivity

  • Two Energy Gaps

  • Interlayer Dynamics

  • Two-band Theory of Electron Doped Cuprates


  1. Topological Quantum Phase Transitions

  • Characterization by String Order Parameters Characterization by String Order Parameters

  • Charge Fractionalization and non-Abelian Anyons
